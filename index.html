My Favourite Hobby Is Playing Cricket

Why???


My hobby is to play cricket with my friends and family. I practice with my dad every day and play a match on the weekend. I play for several teams. These include: Hornsby Reps, Baulkham Hills, West Pennant Hills, Parramatta Reps, NWS Hirricanes Reps, etc. I have played cricket for eight years and have always enjoyed it. From playing cricket, I haven't just enjoyed it, but I improve my physical activity levels. This is essentials for all growing children.
